What is Machine Learning?

Machine Learning (ML) is a core subfield of artificial intelligence that empowers computers to learn directly from data. Instead of relying on strictly written software rules, algorithms analyze massive datasets to discover hidden patterns and improve their performance over time.

Today, ML driving mechanisms are integrated into autonomous vehicles, speech recognition systems, and advanced financial forecasting models. Understanding its distinct paradigms is essential for implementing practical systems.
